The GeoNexus Vision

I wrote this roadmap for myself and for every geotechnical engineer who refuses to be ordinary. Geotechnical engineering is undergoing a quiet revolution. The engineer who once relied solely on traditional theory now competes in a world where numerical modelling, machine learning, physics-informed neural networks, and cloud deployment are becoming table stakes.

Core Philosophy: Theory is our foundation, mathematics our language, and software our tool. At TheGeoNexus, we bridge these with Data Science, Deep Learning, and AI. By integrating GIS, Remote Sensing, and Digital Twins, we move beyond traditional limits to provide intelligence for the ground beneath us—decoding the ground and designing the future with solutions that are truly irreplaceable.

MTech+PhD Roadmap (2026-2030)

A Complete 6-Stage Progression from Fundamentals to Global Recognition.

Download Full Roadmap PDF

This roadmap integrates numerical modelling, AI/ML/Deep Learning, research, and communication. It is built upon three continuous pillars that run in parallel: Engineering Mathematics, AI/ML/Deep Learning, and Communication Skills.

Stage 1: Basic (Foundation Building)

  • Focuses on soil mechanics, Terzaghi consolidation theory, and IS codes.
  • Software tools include Excel, AutoCAD, and Civil 3D intro.
  • AI/ML components cover Python setup, NumPy, Pandas, and linear regression.

Stage 2: Intermediate (Core Software Mastery)

  • Covers retaining wall design, slope stability (LEM), and seepage analysis.
  • Utilizes PLAXIS 2D, GeoStudio, and GEO5.
  • Implements scikit-learn for Random Forest classification and XGBoost for liquefaction prediction.

Stage 3: Advanced (3D Modelling & Deep Learning)

  • Explores advanced constitutive models, rock mechanics, and tunnelling.
  • Requires PLAXIS 3D, ABAQUS, FLAC 2D/3D, and RS2/RS3.
  • Involves Deep Learning with TensorFlow/PyTorch, ANN for settlement prediction, and first PINN prototypes.

Stage 4: Expert (Specialisation, Thesis & Publication)

  • Deep dives into a chosen specialisation track and reliability-based design.
  • Uses DEEPSOIL, OpenSees, Settle3, and PLAXIS Python API.
  • Features full PINN implementation, Bayesian neural networks, and web API deployment using FastAPI and Docker.

Stage 5: Professional (Industry Leadership & Innovation)

  • Focuses on project management, BIM integration, and digital geotechnics.
  • Develops advanced PLAXIS Python scripting and cloud-based simulations.
  • Deploys production ML pipelines, digital twin prototypes, and the GeoNexus platform V2.0.

Stage 6: Apex / Legend (Global Recognition & Platform Building)

  • Aims for international recognition as a Geo-AI expert and numerical modelling authority.
  • Develops novel constitutive models integrating AI and contributes to open-source FEM codes.
  • Achieves 50+ peer-reviewed publications, international keynotes, and a 1000+ user base for the GeoNexus platform.
